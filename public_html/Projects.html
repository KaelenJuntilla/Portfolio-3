Mario is a 2D side scroller game that is a skimp down version of the better and awesome Mario game.At the start you go through a door which then teleports you to another level that has 2 enemies.Between those two enemies is a mushroom.If you collect it you will grow larger and have a double life.This means if you were to be hit by the enemy, you wouldn't die you shrink to your normal height and still be alive.The technologies that were used to create this game are Html, and Css.For more information on these technologies, see my "Technologies Page"
